Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I schedule a porta-potty rental?

Scheduling one to two weeks ahead improves availability, though short-notice rentals may be possible.

What factors influence the cost of porta-potties for rent?

Pricing depends on rental length, unit type, servicing frequency, and site conditions.

How often are porta-potties serviced during the rental period?

Standard servicing usually occurs weekly, with increased frequency for high-traffic use

Do I need a permit for a porta-potty rental?

Permits may be required for public property placements or permitted events.

How many porta-potties should I rent for my event or job site?

The required number depends on attendance size, duration, and usage intensity.
